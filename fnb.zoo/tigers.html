Tigers -

Largest Cats: Tigers are the largest members of the cat family, with some males weighing up to 670 pounds. - Animals: Unlike lions, tigers are generally solitary, with adults having large territories.

-

Hunting: Tigers are stealthy and powerful predators, using their stripes for camouflage to hunt prey like deer and wild boar. -

Tigers inhabit forests and grasslands in Asia, with different subspecies found in various regions.

Social Animals:tigers live in prides, typically consisting of several females, their cubs, and one or more males

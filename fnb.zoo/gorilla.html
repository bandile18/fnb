GORILLA

Let's dive into the fascinating world of gorillas.

Gorillas

-

Physical Characteristics: Gorillas are large primates, with adult males reaching up to 6 feet tall and weighing between 300-400 pounds.

They have thick fur, broad chests, and powerful muscles. - Habitat and Distribution: Gorillas inhabit the forests of central Sub-Saharan Africa,

ivided into two species: eastern gorillas and western gorillas. - Diet and Nutrition: Gorillas are primarily herbivores,

feeding on leaves, shoots, fruits, and stems. They play a crucial role in seed dispersal and forest regeneration.

-

Behavior and Social Structure: Gorillas live in groups, led by dominant males, and exhibit complex social behaviors,

including communication, cooperation, and conflict resolution. - Conservation Status: Gorillas are listed as endangered due to habitat loss, poaching, and disease.

Conservation efforts include habitat protection, research, and community engagement. - Interesting Facts: Gorillas are highly intelligent,

using tools and displaying complex problem-solving skills. They also have unique personalities and can form strong bonds with each other.
